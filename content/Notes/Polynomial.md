#### Definition
A polynomial is a function $p:\mathbb{F} \to \mathbb{F}$ such that $p(z)= a_{0}+a_{1}z+a_{2}z^2 + \cdots + a_{m}z^m$, for all $Z \in \mathbb{F}$.

$P(\mathbb{F})$ is the set of all polynomials with coefficients in $\mathbb{F}$.

The degree of $p\in P(\mathbb{F})$ is $m$ if $p(z) = a_{0}+a_{1}z+\cdots + a_{m}z^m$ and $a_{m} \neq 0$.

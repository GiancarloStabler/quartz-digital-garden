#### Definition
For a [[Morphism of Rings|morphism]] of [[Ring|rings]] $f: (R, +, \cdot) \to (S, +, \cdot)$, we define the kernel of $f$,
$$
\ker f = f ^{-1}(0_{S}) = \{ r\in R \mid f(r)=0_{S} \}.
$$
This is the additive group morphism kernel (it only cares about the additive identity of $S$). We also have that the kernel is a [[subring]] $\ker f \leq R$.

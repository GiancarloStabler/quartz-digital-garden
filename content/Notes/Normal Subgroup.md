#definition #abstract-algebra/group-theory 

#### Definition
A normal [[Subgroup|subgroup]] $N\unlhd G$, is a subgroup $N\leq G$ such that $N$ is closed under [[Conjugate (Groups)|conjugation]] by any element $g\in G$.

Additionally, a normal subgroup $N\unlhd G$ is a subgroup such that the [[Coset|cosets]] of $N$, $gN$ or $Ng$ for all $g\in G$, form a group.

#### Definition
A formula is in conjunctive normal form (CNF) if and only if it has the form,
$$
(\ell_{0,0} \vee \ell_{0,1} \vee \ldots \vee \ell_{0,i_{0}})\wedge (\ell_{1,0} \vee \ldots \vee \ell_{1,i_{1}}) \wedge \ldots \wedge (\ell_{k,0} \vee \ldots \vee \ell_{k,i_{k}}).
$$
Where each $\ell_{a,b}$ is a [[literal]].

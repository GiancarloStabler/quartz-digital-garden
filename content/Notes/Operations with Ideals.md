#note #abstract-algebra/ring-theory 


Given ideals $I,J \unlhd (R, +, \cdot)$, the following are also ideals.
1) $I \cap J$.
2) $I + J = \{  x+y \mid x\in I, y\in J \}$.
3) $I \cdot J = \{ x_{1}y_{1}+x_{2}y_{2}+\ldots+x_{n}y_{n} \mid n \geq 1, x_{i}\in I, y_{i} \in J \}$.

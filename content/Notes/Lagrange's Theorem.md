#theorem #abstract-algebra/group-theory

#### Intuition


#### Formal Statement
For finite [[Group|group]] $G$ and [[Subgroup|subgroup]] $H \leq G$, 
$$
[G : H] = \frac{{\mid G \mid}}{\mid H \mid}.
$$
In particular, note that $\mid H \mid$ divides $\mid G \mid$.

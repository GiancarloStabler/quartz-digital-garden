#definition #abstract-algebra/ring-theory 

#### Definition
For a commutative [[ring]] $R$ with $1$. An [[ideal]] $m \unlhd R$ is maximal if $m \neq R$, and $m \subseteq I \unlhd R$, then $I= m$ or $I = R$.

#### Examples
- In $\mathbb{Z}$, $m \geq 2$ maximal if and only if $m$ is a prime integer.

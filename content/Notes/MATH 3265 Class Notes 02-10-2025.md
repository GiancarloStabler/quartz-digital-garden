#### Reviewed [[Conjunctive Normal Form]] and [[Disjunctive Normal Form]]

#### Theorem

Every formula is equivalent to one in CNF and one in DNF. In fact, for any truth table, there is a formula in CNF, and one in DNF that has that truth table.

#### Defined [[Adequate (for Propositional Logic)]]

We have shown by the CNF/DNF theorem, that $\{\vee, \wedge, \neg\}$ is adequate. Furthermore, $\{ \neg, \wedge \}$ is adequate, as is $\{ \neg, \vee \}$.

#### Definition
For group $G$, and subgroup $H \leq G$, we define the set of all left cosets in $G$ with respect to $H$ as the quotient ${^{\textstyle G}\big/_{\textstyle  H}}$ .

We have that the number of left cosets in $G$ is equal to $\mid {^{\textstyle G}\big/_{\textstyle H}} \mid = [G : H]$ or the index of $H$ in $G$.

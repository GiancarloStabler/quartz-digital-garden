#### Definition
A set of vectors $v_{1},v_{2},\ldots,v_{n} \in V$ are said to be linearly independent if there exist no non-trivial [[linear combination|linear combinations]] of $v_{1},v_{2},\ldots, v_{n}$ equal to the zero vector. 


##### Remark
We say the empty list is linearly independent.

#### Definition
Let $T \in \mathscr{L} (V,W)$, and $v_{1},\ldots, v_{n}$ be a basis of $V$, and $w_{1},\ldots, w_{m}$ by a basis of $W$. The matrix of $T$ with respect to these bases is the $m \times n$ matrix $M(T) = M(T, (v_{1},\ldots,v_{n}), (w_{1},\ldots, w_{m}))$ whose entries $A_{i,j}$ are given by $Tv_{k}=A_{1,k}w_{1}+ A_{2,k}w_{2}+ \cdots + A_{m,k}w_{m}$.
$$
\begin{align*}
M(T) = \begin{matrix}
w_{1} \\
\vdots \\
w_{m}
\end{matrix}&\ \begin{pmatrix}
\ A_{1,k}\   \\
\ \vdots\  \\
\ A_{m,k}\ 
\end{pmatrix} \\
& \begin{matrix}
v_{1} \cdots v_{k} \cdots v_{n}
\end{matrix}
\end{align*}
$$

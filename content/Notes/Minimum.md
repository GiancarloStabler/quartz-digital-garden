#### Definition
If $m$ is a [[#Lower Bound|lower bound]] of a subset $S \subset \mathbb{R}$, and $m \in S$, we call $m$ the (unique) minimum of $S$.

#### Lower Bound
For a subset $S \subset \mathbb{R}$, if $a$ is a real number such that for all $x \in S$, $a \leq x$, we say that $a$ is a lower bound of $S$.

#definition #abstract-algebra 

#### Definition
The kernel of a [[Homomorphism|homomorphism]] $f:A\to B$ is the pre-image in $A$ of the identity element in $B$. The kernel of $f$ is the subset $\ker f \subseteq A$ such that for any $a\in\ker f$, $f(a) = e_B$.

#### Definition
A formula is in disjunctive normal form (DNF) if and only if it has the form,
$$
(\ell_{0,0} \wedge \ell_{0,1} \wedge \ldots \wedge  \ell_{0,i_{0}})\vee (\ell_{1,0} \wedge \ldots \wedge \ell_{1,i_{1}}) \vee \ldots \vee (\ell_{k,0} \wedge \ldots \wedge \ell_{k,i_{k}}).
$$
Where $\ell_{a,b}$ is a [[literal]].

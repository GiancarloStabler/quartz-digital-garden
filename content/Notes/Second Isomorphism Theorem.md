#### Formal Statement
For a group $( G, \cdot )$, with subgroup $H \leq G$, and normal subgroup $N \unlhd G$, we have
$$
{^{\textstyle HN}\big/_{\textstyle N}}\simeq {^{\textstyle H}\big/_{\textstyle H \cap N}}.
$$
Note that $HN = \{ hn \mid h\in H, n\in N \} \leq G$, $N \unlhd HN$, and $H\cap N \unlhd H$.

Furthermore, there exists an isomorphism $F: {^{\textstyle H}\big/_{\textstyle H \cap N}} \to {^{\textstyle HN}\big/_{\textstyle N}}$, defined by $F(h \cdot (H \cap N)) = hN$.

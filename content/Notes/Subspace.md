#definition #abstract-algebra/vector-spaces 

#### Definition
A subset $U$ of $V$ is a subspace of $V$ if $U$ is a vector space with the same $0$, addition, and scalar multiplication as in $V$. 

#### Verifying a Subspace
In order to check that a subset $U \subseteq V$ is a subspace of $V$, we can check the following.
1) $0_{v} \in U$.
2) $u,w\in U$ implies $u+w\in U$ (closure under addition).
3) $u\in U$ and $\lambda \in \mathbb{F}$ implies $\lambda u \in U$ (closure under scalar multiplication).

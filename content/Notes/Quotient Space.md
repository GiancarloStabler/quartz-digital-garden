#definition #abstract-algebra/vector-spaces

#### Definition
If $U$ a subspace of $V$, then the quotient space ${^{\textstyle V}\big/_{\textstyle U}}$ is the vector space of all translates of $U$:
$$
{^{\textstyle V}\big/_{\textstyle U}}= \left\{ v+U : v\in V \right\}.
$$
We can define addition and scalar multiplication on ${^{\textstyle V}\big/_{\textstyle U}}$ as,
$$
(v+U) + (w+ U) = (v+w)+U,
$$
and, 
$$
\lambda (v+U) = (\lambda v) + U.
$$

#### Definition
Let $V_{1},\ldots, V_{n}$ be vector spaces over $\mathbb{F}$. The product is
$$
V_{1} \times \cdots \times V_{n} = \left\{ (v_{1},\ldots,v_{n}) : v_{i} \in V_{i}, 1 \leq i \leq n \right\}.
$$
Addition and scalar multiplication are component-wise:
- $(u_{1},\ldots, u_{n}) + (v_{1}, \ldots, v_{n}) = (u_{1}+v_{1}, \ldots, u_{n}+ v_{n})$.
- $\lambda ( u_{1}, \ldots, u_{n}) = (\lambda u_{1}, \ldots, \lambda u_{n})$.

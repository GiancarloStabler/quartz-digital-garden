#### Defined [[Frobenius Morphism]]
For [[commutative ring]] $R$, $\mathrm{char}R=p \geq 2$, $p$ prime. There exists a [[morphism of rings]] $F$ (the Frobenius morphism) given by,
$$
F: R \to R \text{ such that } F(r)=r^p.
$$
If $R$ is a domain, then $F$ is injective.

##### Binomial Formula
For any commutative ring, we have the binomial formula.

#### Defined [[Formal Derivative]]
For a commutative ring $R$, we have $P(X) = a_{n}X^n +\ldots$

#### Theorem
For $p\geq 2$ prime, $e \geq 1$, $q = p^e$. There exists a field with $q$ elements.

### Representing Linear Maps by a Matrix

We know that if $v_{1}, \ldots, v_{n}$ is a basis of $V$ and $T: V\to W$ is a linear map, then the values of $Tv_{1},\ldots, Tv_{n}$ completely determines the value of $T$ on arbitrary elements of $V$.

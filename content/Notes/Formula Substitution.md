#### Definition
Let $\varphi(p_{0},p_{1},\ldots,p_{n})$ and $\alpha_{0},\alpha_{1},\ldots,\alpha_{n}$ be propositional formulas, then,
$$
\operatorname{Sub}(\varphi(p_{0},p_{1},\ldots,p_{n}))
$$ is the formula obtained by simultaneously substituting each $\alpha_{i}$ in for $p_{i}$.

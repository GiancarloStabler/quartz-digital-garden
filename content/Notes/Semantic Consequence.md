#### Definition
Let $\Gamma$ be a set of propositional formulas (finite or infinite) and let $\varphi$ be a proposition. We say that $\varphi$ is a (semantic) consequence of $\Gamma$ if and only if for every valuation $V$ such that $V \models \Gamma$, we have $V \models \varphi$.

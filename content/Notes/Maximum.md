#### Definition
If $M$ is an [[Maximum#Upper Bound|upper bound]] of a subset $S \subset \mathbb{R}$, and $M \in S$, we call $M$ the (unique) maximum of $S$. 

#### Upper Bound
For a subset $S \subset \mathbb{R}$, if $a$ is a real number such that for all elements $x\in S$, $x \leq a$, we say that $a$ is an upper bound of $S$.

#### Definition
Given an $n\times n$ matrix, we define the characteristic polynomial $f_{A}(x)$ as
$$
f_{A}(x) \overset{def}{=} \det(x \cdot I_{m} - A) = x^n-(\operatorname{tr}A)x^{n-1} + \cdots + (-1)^n \det A
$$

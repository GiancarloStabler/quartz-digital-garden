#definition #abstract-algebra/group-theory 

#### Definition
For a [[Group|group]] $( G, \cdot )$, the exponent of $G$ is the largest exponent $x$ such that $g^x=e_{G}$ for some element $g\in G$. 

Equivalently, for all $y\in G$, $\operatorname{ord}_{}(y) \mid x$.

#### Definition
For a [[Morphism of Rings|morphism]] of [[rings]] $f: (R, +, \cdot) \to (S, +, \cdot)$, we define the image of $f$,
$$
\operatorname{im}f=f(R) = \{  f(r) \mid r \in R \}.
$$
This is the function theoretic image. We also have that the image is a [[subring]] $\operatorname{im} f \leq S$.

#### Remark
If $f$ is a morphism of unital rings, then $f(1_{R})=1_{S} \in \operatorname{im}f$ implies that $\operatorname{im}f$ is a unital subring.

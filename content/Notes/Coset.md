#### Definition
For a group $( G, \cdot )$, subgroup $H \leq G$, and element $g \in G$, we define the left coset of $g$ with respect to $H$ in $G$, as,
$$
gH = \{ gh \mid h \in H \},
$$
and we define the right coset of $g$ with respect to $H$ in $G$, as,
$$
Hg = \{ hg \mid h \in H \}.
$$

#### Properties
1) Distinct left cosets are disjoint, as are distinct right cosets.
2) Distinct left cosets in $G$ partition $G$.
3) If $H$ is finite, then $\mid H \mid = \mid gH \mid = \mid Hg \mid$.

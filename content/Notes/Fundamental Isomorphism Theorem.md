#### Formal Statement
For morphism $f: ( G, \cdot )\to ( H, \circ )$, we have,
$$
\operatorname{im}f \simeq {^{\textstyle G}\big/_{\textstyle \ker f}},
$$
and there exists an isomorphism $F: {^{\textstyle G}\big/_{\textstyle  \ker f}} \to \operatorname{im}f$, defined by $F(g \cdot \ker f) = f(g)$.

#### Corollaries
- Any cyclic group is isomorphic to $\mathbb{Z}$ or $\mathbb{Z}_{n}= {^{\textstyle \mathbb{Z}}\big/_{\textstyle n\mathbb{Z}}}$ for some $n \geq 1$.

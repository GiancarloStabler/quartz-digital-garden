#### Definition
Given a domain $(R,+,\cdot)$, there exists $i:R \hookrightarrow Fr(R)$ (the field of fractions of $R$). It satisfies a "universal property":
$$
\forall K \text{ field}, j:R\hookrightarrow K \text{ an injective morphism} 
$$

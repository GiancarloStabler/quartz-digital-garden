### Limits of Functions

#### Definition
We say $\lim_{ x \to a } f(x)=L$ if $\forall \varepsilon > 0$ there exists $\delta > 0$ such that $\left\lvert f(x)-L \right\rvert < \varepsilon$, for all $x$ such that $\left\lvert x-a \right\rvert < \delta$.

#### Theorem
The limit $\lim_{ n \to \infty }f(n) = L$ if and only if $\forall x_{n}$ sequence so that $x_n \to a$ we have $f(x_n) \to L$.

#definition #abstract-algebra/ring-theory 

#### Definition
Given a [[ring]] $(R, +, \cdot)$ with $1_{R}$, $u\in R$ is a (multiplicative) unit if there exists $v\in R$, such that $uv=vu=1_{R}$.

#### Corollaries
- $R^{\times}=\{ u\in R  \mid u \text{ is a unit}\}$, $( R^{\times}, \cdot )$ is a group (where $\cdot$ is the multiplication in $R$).

#### Definition
For [[group]] $G$, and [[normal subgroup]] $N \unlhd G$, the quotient group of $G$ by $N$ is defined as the group $( {^{\textstyle G}\big/_{\textstyle N}}, \cdot )$.

Importantly, ${^{\textstyle G}\big/_{\textstyle N}}$ carries group structure. This means that $(gN) \bullet (g'N) = (g \cdot g')N$, $e_{{^{\textstyle G}\big/_{\textstyle N}}} =e_{G}N = N$, and $(gN)^{-1}=g^{-1}N$.

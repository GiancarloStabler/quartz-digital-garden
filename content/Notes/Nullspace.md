#definition #abstract-algebra/vector-spaces

#### Definition
For some $T \in \mathscr{L}(V,W)$, the nullspace of $T$, denoted by $\operatorname{null}T$ (or $\ker T$), is the subset of $V$ given by all vectors that map to $0$ under $T$:
$$
\operatorname{null}T = \{  v\in V: Tv=0 \}.
$$
#### Properties
- For map $T:V \to W$, $\operatorname{null}T$ is a subspace of $V$.
